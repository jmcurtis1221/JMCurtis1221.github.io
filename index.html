About Me

Hello, my name is Michael Gary Scott, and this is my art page. I grew up in Scranton, PA, and have spent most of my life working as a branch manager for a mid-range paper supply company. I am considered by many to be the world's best boss, and have made lots of friends in the paper industry, but over the years I realized that art is my true passion. Art has helped me through the toughest times in my life, like when I was Prison Mike, and when I was just a measly salesman. I love art. On this website, I have showcased some of my best work. Much of it is for sale, so feel free to check it out, and if you feel so inclined, contact me about purchasing some art to support a local artist and spruce up your home.
